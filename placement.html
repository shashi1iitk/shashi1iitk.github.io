Placements

The most important piece of information is out.Like every year, this year's placement season saw some records being broken and some being made. As a matter of fact, QS rated IIT Kharagpur as the best institute in India in the Graduate Employability Rankings, ranked 77 in the world. With more than 1100 offerings in 10 days, which took 20 days last year, it won't be an exaggeration to say that IIT Kharagpur saw the biggest job fair in India this year. But does this show the real picture of what the actual scenario looks like. We worked out a detailed study of the inferences and we came up with some very interesting numbers. Numbers without which the placement details cannot be said to be complete. Detailed analysis is coming in upcoming newsletter.
